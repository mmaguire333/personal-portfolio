About Me

Hi there, I'm Matt. I'm a new front-end developer from Colorado. I've always loved building things, and my learning journey through web development has been a creative outlet that has really excited me. I also enjoy solving puzzles. Whether its a rubiks cube, a chess puzzle, or working through a complex program I love using the logical side of my brain to solve challenging problems.

My educational background is in mathematics. In December 2021 I graduated with a BS in Applied Mathematics. What I loved about math was the way in which abstract ideas could be used to build up mathematical structures that can often be used to solve real world problems. This seems to me to be similar to what programming languages do. The similarities between applying the features of a programming language to solve problems and applying results from math to solve problems is something I've found really exciting throughout my learning journey.

Overall, I have really enjoyed my learning journey so far, and I can't wait to see where it will take me next.
